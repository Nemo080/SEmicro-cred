Hospital Playlist



Hosital Playlist revolves around five college friends that become a variety of different doctors. Just like Reply 1988, this drama also focuses on friendship. Even with a busy schedule of being a docter, these five friends still manage to have a close relationship.

Due to this drama focusing on friendship, I enjoyed it just like I did with Reply 1988. It focused on keeping a long lasting friendship even through rough times. I admired their friendship and would definately want to have long lasting relationships with my friends.
