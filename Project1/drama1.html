Reply 1988


Reply 1988 takes place during the 80's with five people that are childhood friends. The drama focuses on the many events that occur between this friend group and their families. It also focuses on events that occured during that time period.

I enjoyed watching this drama as it displayed values that I was able to relate to. For example, it provided with a value of keeping a lasting relationship with both family and friends even after many years go by.
